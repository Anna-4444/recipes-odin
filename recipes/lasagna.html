Lasagna

Description

describe the recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles uncooked
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese shredded and divided
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese shredded and divided
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef on medium-high heat until no pink remains.
  3. Stir in the pasta sauce, italian seasoning and salt and pepper to taste.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine 1 1/2 cups mozzarella, 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, ricotta, parsley, egg, and 1/4 teaspoon salt.
  5. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce in a 9x13 pan. Tip with 3 lasagna noodles. Layer 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture and 1 cup of the meat sauce. Repeat twice more. Finish with remaining 2 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake for 1 hour. Rest for 15 minutes before cutting.
